# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please send an email to our security team at security@example.com. Please include the following information:

- A description of the vulnerability and any relevant details.
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
- Any additional information that might be helpful.

## Response Process

We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide updates on the progress of the resolution. Our security team will assess the submitted vulnerability and take the necessary actions to address it.

## Disclosure Policy

We do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly until they have been resolved. We will coordinate with the reporter to ensure they are kept informed throughout the process.

## Acknowledgments

We thank you for your efforts to improve the security of our project!
